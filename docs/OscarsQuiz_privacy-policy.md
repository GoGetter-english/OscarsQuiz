Oscars Quiz – Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

Oscars Quiz, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when using the Oscars Quiz mobile application.

## Information Collected

When you use the app, we may collect the following information:

## Account Information

If you sign in using email or Google Sign-In, we may collect:

* Email address
* Public display name
* User ID associated with your account

## Gameplay Data

To provide app functionality and leaderboards, we may store:

* Quiz scores
* Answer statistics
* Streak records
* Gameplay progress

## Technical Data

Some technical information may be collected automatically through third-party services used by the app, such as:

* Device type
* App version
* Basic usage data

This information helps improve the reliability and performance of the app.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

* Allow you to create and access an account
* Save your quiz scores and progress
* Display leaderboards and player statistics
* Improve the functionality and performance of the app

We do **not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.**

## Third-Party Services

The app uses trusted third-party services that may process limited user information:

* **Firebase Authentication** – secure login
* **Cloud Firestore** – storage of quiz data and user statistics
* **Google Sign-In** – optional authentication method

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Data is transmitted securely using encrypted connections provided by our backend services.

## Children's Privacy

Oscars Quiz is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
